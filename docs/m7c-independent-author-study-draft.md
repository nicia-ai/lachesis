# Draft M7c independent catalog-author study

Status: **drafted, not executed, identities not frozen**.

M7c may be frozen only after the M7b implementation and report are committed. No
author recruitment, catalog creation, adjudication, provider call, campaign, or
data collection is authorized by this draft.

## Study question

Can independent catalog authors, using only the published packages and a frozen
copy of the public documentation, create and repair versioned semantic-role
declarations and finite conformance suites without allowing false semantic
equivalence?

## Isolation and materials

- Authors are isolated from one another and from Lachesis maintainers during
  authorship and repair attempts.
- Authors receive only packed published packages, their integrity digests, and
  frozen public documentation. They receive no repository source, internal
  fixtures, M7a/M7b corpora, implementation notes, or diagnostic answer keys.
- Every study catalog and role contract is fresh, created after the M7c protocol
  freeze, and was unavailable during M7a and M7b development.
- Catalog strata must include unrelated domains and prespecified semantic
  hazards. The final catalog identities and hidden pair assignments are sealed
  before any author begins.
- Execution is entirely offline. No model or provider is part of authorship,
  diagnosis, repair, or adjudication.

## Blinded adjudication

Two independent semantic adjudicators receive anonymized role contracts, catalog
behavior specifications, and pair identifiers, but not Lachesis output, author
identity, or experimental condition. Each labels every pair genuinely
equivalent, genuinely non-equivalent, or insufficient for adjudication and
localizes the governing obligation. Disagreements go to a third blinded
adjudicator under a prespecified rule. The locked consensus is the reference;
Lachesis diagnostics are revealed only after locking.

## Procedure

1. Record author and catalog stratum assignments and start time.
2. Collect an initial declaration, manifest, suite, and conformance attempt
   before showing structured M7b diagnostics.
3. Reveal the diagnostic for rejected attempts and record comprehension without
   coaching.
4. Permit one prespecified bounded repair attempt; record every proposed edit,
   whether applied, and completion time.
5. Collect the post-diagnostic declaration, manifest, suite, decision, and
   diagnostic identities separately from the initial attempt.
6. Run irrelevant and substantive catalog-evolution tasks from sealed variants
   and record manifest and diagnostic stability.

## Prespecified measures

Report initial-attempt and post-diagnostic results separately for:

- false-equivalence count and rate against blinded adjudication;
- false-rejection count and rate, never recoded as equivalence;
- completion time, including censored non-completions;
- repair attempts, edit type, safe/unsafe direction, and repair success;
- diagnostic comprehension for outcome, role, boundary, obligation, and safe
  next action; and
- manifest and diagnostic identity stability under irrelevant versus substantive
  evolution.

All denominators, missing results, withdrawals, timeouts, and adjudication
uncertainty remain explicit. Report every author-by-catalog stratum plus
prespecified aggregates. No pooling may hide a false equivalence, unsafe repair,
or failing author or catalog stratum.

## Freeze gates

Before execution, freeze sample size and power or precision justification,
author/catalog stratification, randomization, time limits, repair limits,
comprehension rubric, adjudication rules, exclusions, missing-data handling,
analysis code, report schema, and all kill gates.

The primary safety gate remains zero false semantic equivalence overall and in
every author and catalog stratum. Any false equivalence stops the study and is
reported without repair-based recoding. Secondary gates should require zero
unsafe repair directions, complete outcome classification, complete
localization, deterministic reports, and prespecified minimum completion,
comprehension, and stability performance. False rejection is measured separately
and cannot be traded against safety.

## Current decision

**GO to freeze, but NO-GO to run, M7c.** M7b supports final preregistration and
fresh-material preparation. Execution remains unauthorized until identities,
sample justification, adjudicator availability, and the complete protocol are
independently reviewed and frozen.

This draft makes no claim about independent authors, usability, general catalog
equivalence, live inference, strategy promotion, M8, or compositional
generalization.
