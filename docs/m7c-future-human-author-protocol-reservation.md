# Future human-author protocol reservation

No human-author study is included in M7c. M7c's population is fresh, mutually
isolated agent sessions, so its results cannot be described as human-developer
evidence.

A future human-author study requires a new protocol with its own ethical and
consent review, recruitment and compensation plan, eligibility criteria,
sample-size justification, materials, contamination controls, endpoints,
analysis implementation, authorization, registration, and report.

Human observations must use distinct identities and storage and must be reported
separately. They may never be pooled with M7c agent-session outcomes to increase
a denominator, narrow a confidence interval, hide an unsafe agent or human
stratum, or support a combined usability claim.

This reservation authorizes no recruitment, contact, enrollment, or data
collection.
