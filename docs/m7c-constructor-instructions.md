or analyst.

roles, fixture values, and semantic conflicts absent from Lachesis source,

declaration-repairable, and two genuinely non-equivalent pairs with different

variants. Do not encode the class in identifiers, ordering, prose length,

packets contain behavior contracts and registrations but no expected outcome,
repair hint, diagnostic code, or constructor commentary. Seal content digests,
fixture identities, expected class, role, boundary, and obligation before any
author session starts. Submit only opaque identities and counts to the
counts-only auditor.

Any overlap, information leak, identity change, or inability to establish
freshness is a kill-gate failure. Do not replace a failed family after authors
begin.
