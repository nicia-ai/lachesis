Status: **closed-offline-design-no-go**

adversarial audits, and stopped before a live discovery-versus-template study.
No provider call, final corpus, provider identity, campaign, manifest,
preregistration, or spending authority was created.

## Cross-catalog boundary

M6c adds optional trusted `(id, version)` semantic roles to catalog metadata
without changing the plan wire format. Catalog construction rejects duplicate,
dangling, kind-incompatible, and false reducer-law declarations. Catalog
fingerprints bind the declarations.

Declarations alone are not equivalence. The finite offline conformance runner
requires complete role-aligned fixtures and enforces schema acceptance,
role-aligned signatures, deterministic pointwise behavior, reducer identity and
every claimed law, fixed-point and measure behavior, and exact effect authority,
replayability, state semantics, and bounds. Its fresh positive pair used
different catalog/schema/operation IDs. All eight independent hostile mutations
were rejected: **0 accepted hostile collisions out of 8**. The audit digest is
`da038886b4b543c13d1a2f7881d9e7672adb9d52b2964718c44e7fc63a3562c0`.

A passing report is evidence only for its declared finite fixture domain. It
does not rewrite a template's concrete operation references or prove universal
extensional equivalence.

## Paired-study no-go

M6d prospectively defines first-attempt semantic-success noninferiority of
template reuse versus discovery at a 10-percentage-point margin. There is no
prospective discordance distribution, so empirical power remains explicitly
`unknown`. M6 does not reuse its synthetic audits to invent one.

The distribution-free one-sided Hoeffding precision rule requires 600 fresh
pairs in each of two independent repetitions: 1,200 cases total. This exceeds
the 500-case practical ceiling. Effect calls and maximum cost also remain
`unknown` until a concrete catalog effect envelope, provider/model token
envelope, and frozen pricing are supplied. No final corpus was materialized.

The proposed 20-pair canary is reversible and cannot promote automatically.
False role equivalence, authority or budget widening, catalog/report mismatch,
any template-arm planner call, or semantic-contract/replay mismatch disables
reuse and deprecates the template.

## Production decision

M6 closes as experimental offline infrastructure. It does not widen the stable
`@nicia-ai/lachesis-runtime` facade. Cross-catalog operation substitution and a
live study remain unauthorized. A future redesign may change the estimand,
margin, precision rule, repetitions, or practical ceiling prospectively, but a
smaller corpus may not be described as satisfying the frozen M6d design.

## Nonclaims

M6 establishes none of the following:

- learned compositional or cross-domain model generalization;
- universal cross-catalog extensional equivalence;
- accuracy, latency, or cost superiority;
- production-scale template learning or deployment readiness; or
- permission for live inference, provider dispatch, or spending.
